About Us

SKYLINEE LLC was founded with the vision of providing flexible, scalable IT services to both public and private sectors. Led by Corey Jarman, our company brings years of real-world experience in government contracting, systems engineering, and infrastructure deployment.

We specialize in on-site and remote work that includes everything from configuring firewalls to managing complex migrations and cloud integrations—always with a security-first approach.
